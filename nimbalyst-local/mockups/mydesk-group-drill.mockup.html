Three layers, no arbitrary pick: (1) Rank by selector drives the headline number + ▲/▼ and the sort — default Sales, switchable to any KPI, so the arrow column is always comparable. (2) The KPI pulse is 14 diverging micro-bars — one glance tells you "5 of 14 are red and here's roughly how bad," without scanning 14 columns. Hover names each. (3) The worst-decliner chip auto-surfaces each row's single most material decline (weighted by $ impact), so a row with Sales up still flashes ▼ Allowances −14.3% — answering "which KPI declined" per entity. Clicking the chip can sort the whole pane by that KPI.
